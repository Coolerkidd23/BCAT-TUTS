BCom Modules Academy and Tutorial Centre (Hereby BCAT Tuts) is a tutoring organisation for university students, remotely based in Johannesburg we offer our services to students at the University of Johannesburg aswell as students from the University of Limpopo.

BCAT Tuts was initially operating as non-profit organisation since inception on the 18th 0f March 2023 throughout the whole of 2023. We are proud of the success that has been conquered thus far as we started operating as a profit organisation in 2024 till date.

We classify ourselves to be a trusted tutoring partner for university students with a proven track record of constant growth and development. At BCAT Tuts we do not just develop academic weapons and warriors. We develop leaders through both personal and professional growth opprtunities, with academics as the middle activity, bridging knowledge gaps and allowing us to thrive as a community.
