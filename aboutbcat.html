About Us

BCom Modules Academy and Tutorial Centre (BCAT Tuts) is a tutoring organisation for university students. Remotely based in Johannesburg, we offer services to students at the University of Johannesburg and the University of Limpopo.

Our Story of Evolution and Growth

Our journey began in March 2023 as a non-profit, aiming to provide quality tutoring regardless of financial background. In 2024, we transitioned into a profit organization, expanding services and investing in tutor development to reach more students.

Our Mission

To empower future leaders through inclusive, accessible, and collaborative learning experiences that bridge knowledge gaps and unlock potential.

Our Vision

To become a trusted tutoring partner for university students, providing exceptional and affordable education that fosters growth in our communities.

Our Values

We Bridge, We Grow, We Thrive. This embodies our commitment to:

  • Bridging knowledge gaps and making education accessible.
  • Growing minds and fostering personal development.
  • Thriving together as a community of learners.

At BCAT Tuts, we don't just develop academic warriors. We develop leaders through personal and professional growth opportunities, with academics as the core activity, bridging knowledge gaps and enabling community growth.
