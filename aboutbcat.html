About us!!

BCom Modules Academy and Tutorial Centre (Hereby BCAT Tuts) is a tutoring organisation for university students, remotely based in Johannesburg we offer our services to students at the University of Johannesburg aswell as students from the University of Limpopo.



Our Story of Evolution and Growth

At BCAT Tuts, we're passionate about empowering students to achieve academic excellence. Our journey began in March 2023, when we founded the organization as a non-profit entity. Our mission was to provide high-quality tutoring services to students in need, regardless of their financial background.

As we navigated our first year, we realized that our impact could be even greater if we adopted a more sustainable business model. In 2024, we made the strategic decision to evolve into a profit organization. This transition enabled us to expand our services, invest in our tutors' development, and reach even more students.

Our Mission

To empower future leaders through inclusive, accessible, and collaborative learning experiences that bridge knowledge gaps and unlock potential.

Our Vision

to become a trusted tutoring partner for university students, providing them with exceptional and affordable education that fosters growth in our communities.

Our Values

We live by our slogan: We Bridge, We Grow, We Thrive. These words embody our commitment to:

- Bridging knowledge gaps and making education accessible.

- Growing minds and fostering personal development.

- Thriving together as a community of learners.

With a proven track record of constant growth and development, at BCAT Tuts we do not just develop academic weapons and warriors. We develop leaders through both personal and professional growth opprtunities, with academics as the middle activity, bridging knowledge gaps and allowing us to thrive as a community.
